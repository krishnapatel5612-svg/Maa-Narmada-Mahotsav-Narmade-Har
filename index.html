MYTHOLOGY


Maa Narmada ka Janm


Purano ke anusar, Maa Narmada ka janm Bhagwan Shiv ke kathin tap aur unke shareer se nikale pasine (sweat) se hua tha. Isliye inhe 'Shankari' bhi kaha jata hai.


Maa Narmada ne var manga tha ki vo hamesha pavitra rahengi aur unke darshan matra se hi bhakton ke paap dhul jayenge. Yahi karan hai ki Ganga snan ka jo mahatva hai, wahi Narmada darshan ka hai.

Pavan Shlokas

spiritual wisdom

"नर्मदे सर्वदे देवी, नमामि देवी नर्मदे।"

He Maa Narmada, jo sab kuch dene wali ho, main aapke charno mein baar-baar pranam karta hoon.

"पुण्यसलिला नर्मदा, स्मरणात पाप-नाशिनी।"

Pavitra jal wali Maa Narmada ka keval smaran karne se hi janm-janmantar ke paap nashta ho jate hain.

"त्वदीय पाद पंकजम, नमामि देवी नर्मदे।"

Maa, main aapke kamal ke saman komal charno mein vandan karta hoon, jo bhay aur paap ka nash karte hain.

"रेवा तट निवसिनी, महा पाप विनाशिनी।"

Rewa (Narmada) ke tat par nivas karne wali Maa, bade se bade paapon ka vinash karti hain.

Narmada Jayanti Pujan Vidhi

  • Pavitra Snan (Morning)

    Suryoday se pahle Narmada tat par snan karein. Agar tat par jana sambhav na ho, to ghar par hi jal mein Narmada jal milakar snan karein.

  • Sankalp aur Abhishek

    Maa Narmada ki pratima ya chitra ke samne baithkar pujan ka sankalp lein. In ka panchamrit aur jal se abhishek karein.

  • Chunari Manorath

    jayanti ke din Maa ko Chunari aur saubhagya samagri arpit karne ka bada mahatva hai. Isse manokamna poori hoti hai.

  • Deep Daan (Evening)

    Sham ko Narmada ji ke tat par ya mandir mein 108 diye (ya samarthya anusar) jalakar deep-daan karein. Ye jeevan mein prakash lata hai.

  • Aarti aur Prasad

    Ant mein "Maa Narmada Aarti" karein aur halwe ka bhog lagakar bhakton mein prasad vitaran karein.
